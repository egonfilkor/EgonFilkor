About me

Egon Filkor

I've been shooting mostly outdoor sports and travel photos for over 6 years. I also make videos on similar themes, and I record and broadcast sporting events. I use a MILC camera frame and 3 lenses. The subjects I capture are in line with my interests, as I have been playing football for more than a decade and my studies have led me to focus on the technical side of motor sport.
